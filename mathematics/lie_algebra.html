Lie Groups

Lie groups $G$ are manifolds equipped with two operations:
  1. Group multiplication: $\mu: G \times G \rightarrow G $
  2. Inversion: $inv: G \rightarrow G$

Left Invariant vector fields

A vector field $X$ on $G$ is called left-invariant if \begin{align} X_p = (L_p)_* X_e \end{align} Where $L_p: G \rightarrow G; L_p(g) = \mu(p,g) = pg$
