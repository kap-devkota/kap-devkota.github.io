Covariant Derivatives of a Lie group

Let $X, Y, Z$ be left-invariant field in a Lie Group $G$. To obtain the covariant derivative $\nabla_Y X$:

\begin{align} 2\langle \nabla_Y X, Z \rangle &= (L_X g)(Y, Z) + d\theta_X (Y, Z) \\ &= D_X (g(Y, Z)) - g([X, Y], Z) - g(Y, [X, Z]) + \partial_k g_{ij} X^i (Y^k Z^j - Y^j Z^k) + g_{ij} \partial_k X^i (Y^k Z^j - Y^j Z^k) \\ &= -g([X, Y], Z) - g(Y, [X, Z]) + (-g_{ij}Y^k \partial_k (X^i Z^j) +g_{ij}Z^k \partial_k (X^i Y^j) + g_{ij}\partial_kX^i Y^k Z^j - g_{ij}\partial_kX^i Y^jZ^k ) \\ &= -g([X, Y], Z) - g(Y, [X, Z]) + (-g_{ij}Y^k X^i \partial_k (Z^j) +g_{ij}Z^kX^i \partial_k ( Y^j) ) \\ &= -g([X, Y], Z) - g(Y, [X, Z]) - g(X, [Y, Z]) \end{align}
